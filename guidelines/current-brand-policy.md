# Silver Tree current brand policy — 2026-05-01

## Purpose

This file defines the current operating policy for Silver Tree Coffee Roaster / silvertree coffee. Use it when creating or revising SNS posts, product descriptions, visuals, and internal guidelines.

## Current brand direction

Silver Tree should be operated as a specialty coffee focused brand.

Primary value points:

- specialty coffee quality
- fresh roasting
- aroma, sweetness, aftertaste
- clear explanation of origin, process, roast level, and flavor
- small omikuji experience for gifts

## Keep

- specialty coffee
- White Blend / Dark Blend
- single origin coffees
- coffee gifts
- omikuji as a small gift experience
- roasting, brewing, and origin education
- natural BASE shop flow

## Do not reuse as the main message

Treat the following as old context. Do not use them as the default direction for new posts or product pages.

- letter service
- future-letter concept
- time capsule concept
- four-minute recovery concept
- read-one-minute / drink-three-minutes concept
- ume / take / matsu product tiers
- CAMPFIRE-centered announcements
- travel-coffee-shop wording used by inertia

Exceptions require explicit user confirmation.

## Recommended positioning

```text
Silver Tree is a self-roasting specialty coffee brand that makes high-quality coffee easier to understand, easier to choose, and easier to give as a gift.
```

## Gift positioning

```text
A specialty coffee gift with a small omikuji. The coffee remains the main value; the omikuji adds a small moment of enjoyment.
```

## SNS direction

- explain origins in simple language
- explain roast level and flavor differences
- compare White Blend and Dark Blend clearly
- show how the coffee works as a gift
- treat omikuji as a small extra, not the main product

## Pre-post checklist

- Does the post avoid returning to the old letter-service concept?
- Does the post avoid old recovery and travel-centered language unless intentionally approved?
- Does the post avoid ume / take / matsu tiers?
- Does the post clearly look like a specialty coffee brand?
- Is omikuji positioned as a small gift extra?
- For knowledge posts, was `.claude/skills/fact-check-infographic/SKILL.md` followed?

## x-post-log.md handling

`x-post-log.md` contains old posts as history. Do not delete old history only to hide it. For future posts and revised posts, use this current policy.
